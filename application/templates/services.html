Coming Soon: QA Cafe Lux - Prepare your taste buds for an exciting addition to QA Cinemas – the
QA Café Lux. Soon, you'll be able to savour a delightful array of culinary delights in our indoor
restaurant. From gourmet burgers to mouth-watering pasta dishes, our menu will cater to

Café Lux will provide a cosy and delicious dining experience for you and your companions.

QA Indoor Arcade At QA Cinemas, the fun doesn't stop when the credits roll. Step into
QA-cade, our indoor arcade, and discover a world of entertainment beyond the big screen.
We have an assortment of classic and modern arcade games, perfect for kids and adults
alike. Challenge your friends to a game of air hockey, test your skills on the claw machines,
or try your luck at our selection of video games. QA-cade promises hours of amusement and
is an excellent way to pass the time before or after your movie.

Accessibility and Comfort We care about your comfort during your visit. Our cinemas are
designed to be accessible to all, with spacious seating arrangements and dedicated areas for
patrons with special needs. We also offer hearing-impaired devices and closed captioning
services for select screenings.

Loyalty Program Don't forget to inquire about our loyalty program, which rewards our
frequent visitors with exclusive discounts, free tickets, and special event invitations. Your
loyalty is highly valued, and we want to express our appreciation in meaningful ways.
