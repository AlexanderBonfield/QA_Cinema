Who We Are

QA Cinemas is a distinguished cinema chain with a rich legacy spanning over two decades
since its establishment in 2004. Renowned in the film industry, our cinemas are celebrated
for their unique blend of contemporary blockbusters and exclusive screenings of beloved
classics from the 1960s to the 1990s.
Our journey began in the vibrant Northwest of England, where we have enjoyed remarkable
success. Looking ahead, we are excited to announce our ambitious expansion plans, set to
unfold on a national scale in the upcoming years.

Meet Our Team

Our team is a group of dedicated individuals who share a common passion and a relentless

Each member of our team brings their unique expertise and perspective to the table, but
