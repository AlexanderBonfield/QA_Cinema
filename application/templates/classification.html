Film Classifications

These classifications are designed to help viewers make informed choices about the films
they watch and ensure that content aligns with their preferences and the age-
appropriateness for themselves or their children. G (General Audiences): Suitable for all ages. Contains no content that would offend parents
if viewed by children. PG (Parental Guidance): Some material may not be suitable for children. Parents are urged
to provide "parental guidance." NC-17 (Adults Only): No one 17 and under admitted. This rating signifies that the film
contains explicit sexual content or adult material that is not suitable for minors.
